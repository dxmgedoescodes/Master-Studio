# Master Studio — The Best Upcoming DAW

Master Studio is an ambitious, modern digital audio workstation (DAW) built for creators who want pro-level sound and an intuitive workflow. Designed from the ground up for low-latency tracking, effortless composition, and collaborative production, Master Studio brings powerful features, smart automation, and a future-forward roadmap — all packaged to become the go-to DAW for the next generation of producers.

> "Master Studio is crafted to feel like an instrument itself — fast to learn, deep to master."

---

## Highlights — Why Master Studio is the best upcoming DAW
- Fast, fluid workflow focused on musical ideas, not menus.
- Pro-quality audio engine engineered for low latency and high fidelity.
- Built-in instruments and effects that cover production to mastering.
- Collaboration-ready: share and work on projects with others easily.
- Scales from bedroom producers to studio teams with flexible plans and cloud features.

---

## Key Features
- Unlimited audio & MIDI tracks, flexible routing and bussing.
- Low-latency audio engine with high-quality sample-rate support.
- Advanced MIDI editing: comping, step, piano-roll, and pattern-based workflows.
- Built-in instruments: synths, sampled instruments, drum machines, and a modular rack.
- Professional effects: EQ, compressors, reverbs, delays, saturation, and mastering chain.
- VST/AU plugin hosting with sandboxing for stability.
- Smart templates, AI-assisted track suggestions, and quick-presets to speed workflow.
- Offline and real-time rendering with bounce options and stems export.
- Automation lanes that are easy to edit and draw; clip-based modulation.
- Collaboration: cloud-backed project sync, version history, and live co-editing (coming soon).
- Cross-platform support with consistent project compatibility.

## Plans (Designed for every creator)
Master Studio will offer flexible plans so you can pick what fits your workflow and budget. Pricing shown is introductory and subject to change.

- Free
  - Basic audio and MIDI recording
  - 8 instrument/effect plugin slots
  - Basic export (stereo)
  - Community support
  - Perfect for trying the core experience

- Creator (monthly / yearly)
  - Unlimited tracks
  - Expanded instruments & effects library
  - Multi-format exports (stems, multitrack)
  - Basic cloud project sync
  - Priority community support

- Pro (monthly / yearly)
  - All Creator features
  - Full plugin hosting (VST/AU) and plugin sandboxing
  - Advanced mastering chain & metering tools
  - Higher-quality offline rendering & DDP export
  - Early access to beta features

- Studio / Enterprise (contact sales)
  - Team collaboration & project management
  - Dedicated cloud storage & backups
  - Enterprise support & SLAs
  - On-prem / private-hosted options (for large studios)

Note: Exact pricing, trial durations, and plan specifics will be finalized during the beta phase. If you'd like, we can add a pricing table or a feature-comparison chart.
